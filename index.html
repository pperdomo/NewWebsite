About

Get To Know Me.

My favorite way to describe myself is "visual communicator." Behind the best designs there are great stories. I like to create those stories and listen to them. It's how my designs work with purpose, to showcase awesome graphics through a multitude of platforms, like websites, print collateral, videos and more.

I like to challenge processes that have been done the same way for a long time. Innovation is key when the goal is long-lasting change. That is what I hope to continue doing as my career progresses, to create materials that make a positive and forward-looking difference.

Right now, I am working to consistently grow in my technical design and communication skills, to foster a continuously learning mentality. Being the first college graduate in my family has shown me what it takes to reach my goals and how to be motivated. I strive to put that same persevering, inquisitive spirit into all my designs. Let's work together and bring your ideas to life!

Services

What I Do.

I am your resident design nerd. These are the methods I use to make each project and collaboration a success.

  • Branding

    It is everything. Through the creation of logos and stylesheets, I make your company and product stand out. Sticking to these brand guidelines helps place your product in the customer's mind and makes it recognizeble with every new project we deliver. My goal is to create a package for you.

  • Graphic Design

    Anything from vector graphics, presentations(i.e. Powerpoint/Prezi), promotional materials (brochures, flyers) and photography/video editing. I use typography and composition to make these materials push your ideas further.

  • Web Design

    Dynamic and static promotional banners for web display and/or social media platforms. I can create websites through CMS systems (Wordpress/Sharepoint) and web development (HTML/CSS/JS).

  • Project Management & Organization

    The range of these projects means there are many happening at once. It would be extremely diffucult to keep track without a system. I use colorful spreadsheets and productivity suites like Slack and Trello to stay efficient.
